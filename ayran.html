Ingredients (Serves 4)

500g ground lamb (preferably with 20% fat) 1 medium onion, finely grated 2 cloves of garlic, minced 1 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes (optional for spiciness) 1 teaspoon paprika Salt to taste Freshly ground black pepper Flat metal skewers (preferably wide kebab skewers) Optional: Fresh parsley or chili peppers for garnish

Preparation

Place the ground lamb in a large bowl. Add the grated onion, minced garlic, crushed red pepper flakes, paprika, salt, and black pepper. Mix the ingredients thoroughly by hand for at least 5-7 minutes until the mixture becomes sticky and well combined. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to blend. Preheat a grill or charcoal barbecue. Take portions of the mixture and mold them onto the skewers, forming flat and even kebab shapes. Wet your hands slightly to prevent sticking. Grill the kebabs over high heat, turning occasionally, until nicely charred and fully cooked (about 8-10 minutes). Serve hot with flatbread, grilled vegetables, fresh parsley, and optionally spicy green peppers or sumac onions.
